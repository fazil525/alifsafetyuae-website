Frequently Asked Questions

Are your training courses KHDA approved?

Yes, our company works under the strictest UAE regulations and our courses align with local authority approvals, including KHDA standards.

Do you provide on-site training at our facility?

Absolutely. We can dispatch our expert trainers directly to your facility or construction site to conduct hands-on, practical training tailored to your environment.

How long does a third-party inspection take?

The duration depends on the scope and complexity of the equipment or site. Most standard lifting and machinery inspections can be completed within 1-2 business days with reports generated shortly after.
